Village Moving Service

1 Month Before Move

● Make a budget for all moving expenses including new furnishing and appliances.
● Make a plan to transition your children into their new schools, including getting their transcripts ready.
● Create a Manifest of everything you want to bring to either your new home or storage facility.
● Start donating, giving away, or selling items that you will not be keeping.
● Make sure whatever furniture/appliances you are bringing over or buying will fit through the door of your new home.
● Gather all your important documents in a single place.

4 Weeks Before Move

● Notify your housing/renter insurance agent about your move.
● Notify your banks and credit agencies about your move, and your new address.
● Notify the United States Postal Service of your change of address so you do not lose any mail.

3 Weeks Before Move

● Schedule an appointment with your new cable and Internet Service Provider.
● Start packing some items, so you have less to do in the future.
● By the end of this week, you should have a finalized manifest on where your items will be moved to.

2 Weeks Before Move

● Stay in contact with us and make sure that your move date is set in stone.
● Create a schedule for your move, our team will assist you in estimating how long the whole move process happens.
● Plan your work and social schedule around your move date. Specific incidents can cause prolonged move times, so make sure you free more than enough time for your move.
● Notify your bank, credit institutions, car insurance, and utility companies about your move.
● Forward your mail through USPS.

Week Of Move

● Before our team arrives, items should be wrapped and packed in boxes ready to be transitioned into the truck.
● Any loose items that have to be wrapped during the move day will cause an increased length that will differ from the original estimate.
● During the move, our team will take care of wrapping and packing all of your furniture.
● We will make sure the actual day of your move goes as smooth as possible and will not stop working for you until all of your tasks are completed.
